About US

We're an entirely student run and student managed band, we rehearse outside of school. We put in our own time and effort without any control from our school.

We are a primarily brasshouse band, although we like to play all sorts of jazz.

This band was started when Olivier started asking some of his friends if they were interested in playing FunkyTown together. After having such a good reception at our first performance, we decided to stay together, and branch out into more music.
